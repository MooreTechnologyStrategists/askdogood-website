Your Week 1 Healing Gameplan

Based on the AskDoGood Week-1 checklist – one small focus per day so you don’t burn out on Day 2.

  1. Day 1 – Learn Your Thyroid

    Read the Thyroid 101 guide and understand what’s actually going on in your body.

  2. Day 2 – Clean Up Your Plate

    Do a simple pantry audit. Reduce processed foods, add in greens, beans and whole grains.

  3. Day 3 – Stress Check

    Identify the top 3 stress triggers and pick one calming ritual (breathing, prayer, stretching, music).

  4. Day 4 – Sleep Reset

    Cut late-night scrolling, create a wind-down routine, and aim for a consistent bedtime.

  5. Days 5–7 – Practice & Reflect

    Repeat the basics, track how you feel, and plan your next 30 days with the 7-Day Thyroid Reset guide.
